Who We Are

CostiQ Estimation is a professional construction cost estimating company serving contractors, developers, architects, and construction firms worldwide. We specialize in delivering accurate, detailed, and reliable cost estimates that help our clients plan confidently and win more bids.

With a strong understanding of construction drawings, specifications, and current market pricing, our team ensures every estimate reflects real-world costs and project requirements.

What We Do

We provide comprehensive construction estimating services for residential, commercial, and mixed-use projects. Our services include material takeoffs, BOQ preparation, pre-tender estimates, and budget planning tailored to each client’s needs.

Using industry-standard software such as PlanSwift, Bluebeam, and advanced Excel spreadsheets, we analyze drawings and specifications in detail to deliver precise and well-structured estimates.

Our Commitment

From small residential builds to large commercial developments, we are committed to accuracy, consistency, and confidentiality. Every project is handled with professionalism and strict attention to detail to ensure client satisfaction.
