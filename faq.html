Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit my construction plans?
You can upload your drawings directly through our contact or quote form. We accept most common formats.
What file formats do you accept?
PDF, DWG, PNG, JPG. Make sure your drawings are clear and legible for accurate estimation.
What is the typical turnaround time?
Standard projects are delivered within 24–48 hours. Larger or complex projects may take longer, and we will notify you.
How accurate are your estimates?
Our estimates have a proven 98–99% accuracy rate based on previous project audits. Every estimate is quality-checked before delivery.
Do you provide MEP & trade-specific estimates?
Yes, we provide detailed BOQs, takeoffs, and cost estimates for all CSI divisions including MEP, finishes, and structural work.
